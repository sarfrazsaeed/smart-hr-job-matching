How It Works

From Job Post to Top Candidates
in Three Steps

1

Post Your Job

HR enters the job title, required skills (comma-separated), minimum experience years, and job type. Takes under 60 seconds.

2

Register Candidates

Candidates add their skills, education, and experience. Skill tag chips make input fast. All data stores client-side instantly.

3

Run the Match

One click runs the weighted algorithm. Candidates are ranked by score with matched/missing skills clearly visualised per row.
