Process

How SmartHire Works

Three steps from job posting to ranked candidates.

1
Post a Job

HR manager goes to HR Portal, enters job title, required skills (comma separated), minimum years of experience, and job type.

2
Candidates Register

Candidates fill in their name, email, skills, education, and years of experience on the Candidate Registration page.

3
Algorithm Scores Everyone

The matching engine compares each candidate against the job. Skills matched ÷ skills required × 70 + experience score × 20 + education × 10.
