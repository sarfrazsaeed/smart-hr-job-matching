About SmartHire

A smart HR & job matching system built with modern web technologies

Candidate Registration

Register with name, email, skills, education, and experience. Duplicate email prevention built in.

Job Posting Portal

HR managers can post jobs with required skills, experience level, and job type.

Smart Skill Matching

Match candidates to any job with a percentage match score showing how well each candidate fits.

Live Dashboard

View total candidates, jobs, unique skills, and average experience. See top skills distribution.
